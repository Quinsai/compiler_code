CompUnit → {Decl} {FuncDef} MainFuncDef
Decl → 'const' ... | 'int' ...
FuncDef → 'void' ... | 'int' ... '('
MainFuncDef → 'int' 'main' '('

一次性偷看三个单词
'const' ** ** -> Decl
'void' ** ** -> FuncDef
'int' 'main' '(' -> MainFuncDef
'int' ** '(' -> FuncDef
others -> Decl
